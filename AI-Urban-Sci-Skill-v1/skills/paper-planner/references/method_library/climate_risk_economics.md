## Flood Underinsurance Protection Gap Estimation

- **Paper**: Measuring flood underinsurance in the USA (Nature Climate Change, 2025)
- **DOI**: 10.1038/s41558-025-02396-w
- **Domain**: climate_risk_economics

- **Outcome type**: continuous
- **Inferential target**: distributional_pattern

### Method specification
- **Method name**: Protection gap estimation from exceedance probability curves with NFIP policy data and FSF damage estimates
- **Hyperparameters**: null — underspecified in paper
- **Required validations**: tract-level income and minority composition analysis, sensitivity to coverage limit assumptions, comparison of insured share across socioeconomic deciles
- **Characteristic outputs**: annual protection gap (US$17.1 billion uninsured of total losses), underinsurance estimate (US$15.7 billion annually), 88% of at-risk households underinsured, average underinsurance US$7,208 per year, tract-level distribution by income and minority composition
